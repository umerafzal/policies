2. Description of Service

iBuyHalal is a mobile application designed to help users identify whether food products are halal (permissible), haram (forbidden), or mushbooh (doubtful) according to Islamic dietary guidelines. The App provides the following features:

  • Barcode Scanning: Scan product barcodes to identify ingredients and determine halal status
  • OCR Technology: Extract ingredients from product packaging using text scanning
  • Ingredient Analysis: Match ingredients against a database of halal/haram ingredients
  • Scan History: View your previous product scans and determinations
  • Statistics: Track your scanning activity and halal status determinations
