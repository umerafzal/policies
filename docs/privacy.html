Privacy Policy

iBuyHalal - Halal Product Verification App

Last Updated: January 17, 2025

Effective Date: This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 17, 2025 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will take effect immediately upon being posted on this page.

1. Introduction

Welcome to iBuyHalal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our mobile application iBuyHalal (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Our App is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the App.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Scan History: We store your product scan history locally on your device, including:
    • Barcode numbers of scanned products
    • Product names (if available)
    • Halal status determination (Halal, Haram, Mushbooh, or Not Found)
    • Flagged ingredients and their details
    • Timestamp of each scan
  • Usage Statistics: We track aggregate statistics locally on your device, including:
    • Total number of scans
    • Number of halal, haram, and mushbooh determinations
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage analytics, including:
    • When you scan a barcode and view results
    • When you provide feedback (thumbs up/down)
    • When you request more details about a product
    • App usage patterns and feature interactions

    Note: Analytics data is anonymized and does not include personally identifiable information.

2.3 Device Information

We may collect certain device information automatically, including:

  • Device type (iOS or Android)
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (anonymized)

2.4 Camera Access

Our App requires access to your device's camera to scan product barcodes and ingredient lists. Camera access is used solely for this purpose, and we do not store, transmit, or share any images captured by your camera. All image processing occurs locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Our Service: To enable barcode scanning, ingredient analysis, and halal status determination
  • To Maintain Scan History: To allow you to view your previous scans and product determinations
  • To Improve Our App: To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality and user experience
  • To Provide Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App and identify areas for improvement
  • To Update Database: To check for and download updated product databases from our servers

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All scan history and statistics are stored locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms (AsyncStorage and SQLite). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

4.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process data:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): We use Google's Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. This service is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Google uses data at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • GitHub: We download product databases from GitHub repositories. This is a one-way data transfer (download only), and no personal information is sent to GitHub.

4.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Analytics Providers: We share anonymized usage analytics with Firebase Analytics (Google) as described above
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access your scan history stored locally on your device
  • Delete your scan history at any time through the App's settings
  • Clear all stored data by uninstalling the App

6.2 Camera Permissions

You can revoke camera access at any time through your device's settings. Note that revoking camera access will prevent you from using the barcode scanning feature.

6.3 Analytics Opt-Out

While we use Firebase Analytics, you can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. However, basic analytics may still be collected to improve app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

Specifically, analytics data may be processed by Google's servers, which may be located outside your country of residence.

9. Data Retention

We retain your scan history and statistics on your device until you choose to delete them or uninstall the App. Analytics data is retained by Firebase Analytics according to Google's data retention policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
